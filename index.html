We're Back
and better than ever!

To make 2067 an even better year, GREYDEY is enticing OG greyhound racing fans to help us bring back the beloved sport, With a twist!
It’s historically known that thousands of greyhounds were mistreated. Suffering injures and hundreds being put down, leading NZ to ban racing in 2029. Since then, the number of illegal racing has crept up. At GREYDEY, we have redesigned the old experience to eliminate the harm it caused and create new sense of thrill and excitement. We are bring back the social aspect that punters would have missed from the original sport. We have developed state of the art robotic technology that clones and mimics the handlers trained dogs. The robotic clones are connected to the control deck that handlers command. As a spectator, you’ll be able to experience the highs and lows of the original sport, while also being able to view what the original dogs are up to. Giving you a juxtaposition of calm and chaotic. There will be variables thrown at the roboDogs that you wouldn’t necessarily see in the original sport. But don’t just come for the race, stay for the puppy room, adoption centre and the ability to reminisce on what the sport was like, with this futuristic twist that will make you question how this sport was allowed for so long?
